ARN application

What is an ARN?
An ARN is an Aviation Reference Number. It is similar to an account number or customer number and it should be quoted whenever you communicate with CASA. It is your own unique identifier

Individuals

How do I apply for my ARN?
First you'll need to register an account by verifying your email address.
Once your email address has been verified, you'll be able to access the new digital ARN application to be issued your ARN

Do I have to apply for an ARN online?
No, you can still download form 1162 to complete and submit via email to applications@casa.gov.au
